04 · The Strategy

How buyers actually buy

Get this right and your home doesn't compete on price — buyers compete for it. Here's how it works.

Buyer journey · 1 of 6

Every buyer starts with a circle.

They choose the area they want to live in — and only look inside the circle. Your home is already inside someone's right now.

Buyer journey · 2 of 6

Then they line them up by price.

Inside that circle, they line up everything for sale and sort it cheapest to dearest.

Buyer journey · 3 of 6

They offer from the cheapest up.

Offer, miss out, move to the next — working up in price until something fits.

"Missed out" = lost to a stronger buyer, not unaffordable.

Buyer journey · 4 of 6

But they stretch for the standout.

Buyers always pay more for the home that presents the best. That's the one they fall for.

Buyer journey · 5 of 6

My job: make yours the standout.

I market your home so it stops being just another option — and becomes everyone's first pick.

Buyer journey · 6 of 6

Then we negotiate from strength.

With buyers competing and stretching, I'm not chasing one offer — I'm choosing the best one.
